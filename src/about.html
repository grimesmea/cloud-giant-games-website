Cloud Giant Games is a husband and wife team who fell in love over a rousing game of 'Tales of Vesperia'. With Mary pursuing a degree in music performance and Dan a degree in computer science, they made their first game based on a silly idea, one key, and a catchy tune.

We continue to make games together collaboratively and have some fun projects in the works that are designed to be experienced in virtual reality.
